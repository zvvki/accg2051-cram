The 10-mark problem question

It's drawn from the tutorials: apply the law to a scenario, or explain an area of law. Use IRAC.

IRAC structure

Issue — the precise legal question the facts raise (e.g. “Is Sailaway bound by the mortgage?”).

Rule — the law: the relevant Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) section(s) + the leading case that established the principle.

Application — apply the rule to the facts; argue both sides where the facts are unclear; point to the exact facts that satisfy or fail each element.

Conclusion — a clear answer + the likely outcome/remedy or advice.
